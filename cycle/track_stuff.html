Basic riding

  • Your bike
  • Your bike's ignition key (very important!)
  • Full tank of gas in bike
  • Leathers
  • Boots
  • Gloves
  • Helmet
  • Back protector
  • Earplugs

Essentials for a road trip

  • Palm or PocketPC with AvantGo®
  • Maps to get to and from the track
  • Cash
  • Credit card
  • A pen and paper
  • Driver's license
  • Mobile phone
  • Changes of clothes
  • Toiletries

Comfort/Health

  • Sunscreen
  • Lip balm
  • Drinking water
  • Snacks
  • Salt and potassium sources (food, pills, or drinks with electrolytes)
  • Awning (with weights for the legs if necessary)
  • Chairs
  • Shoes and extra socks for walking around between sessions
  • Ibuprofen, acetaminophen, and/or aspirin
  • Sweatshirt or jacket
  • Coat hanger for your leathers
  • Baseball cap
  • Sunglasses
  • List of emergency numbers that someone else will be able to find easily

Nice things to have

  • Swimsuit (in case you come across a hot tub or pool at the hotel)
  • Camera
  • Camcorder with tapes, batteries, and battery charger
  • Push scooter
  • Flashlight
  • Lantern
  • A walkman, lots of tapes, and a long book (in case you crash out)
  • DC-to-AC inverter (12 volts DC to 120 volts AC)

Bike repairs and supplies

  • Toolbox containing the following:
    • Wrenches (hex and allen)
    • Screwdriver (both Phillips and flathead -- flat is good for prying)
    • A pocketknife
    • Cable ties
    • Tire pressure gauge
    • Duct tape
    • Electrical tape
    • Extra nuts, bolts, fender washers, crush washers
  • Bike stand if your bike needs one
  • Jumper cables
  • Enormous gas can with gasoline
  • Motor oil
  • Clear contact paper for taping up your bike's street crap
  • Extra tiedowns (in case someone who rode in crashes)
  • Glass cleaner and paper towels
  • Shop towels or rags
